🎨 Aesthetic & Palette Description
Color Palette
Dominant Colors: Bright sky blue (azure), lush vibrant green (emerald and moss), and warm sun-kissed gold.

Accents: Soft terracotta, ancient stone gray, and luminous teal or pastel pink (for magical/ancient elements).

Lighting: Warm golden-hour sunlight, soft bloom effects, god rays (crepuscular rays) filtering through clouds or trees, and a gentle overhead glow that makes environments feel safe and hopeful.

Art Style Keywords
Vibe: Whimsical, adventurous, serene, evocative, and full of wonder.

Visual Style: Stylized 2D/3D hybrid, painterly textures, cel-shaded art, clean line work, and soft gradients.
