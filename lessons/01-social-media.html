English Conversation Lesson

Is Social Media Making Us Lonely?

Level: A1 – B1 • Time: 55 min
Fase 1 — Hook
5 minutes
"You have 500 friends online.
How many can you call at 3 a.m.?"

What do you think? — Today we talk about this.

Fase 2 — Vocabulário-chave
10 minutes

Key Vocabulary

1 Read out loud and repeat after the teacher:

WordPronunciationMeaningUse it
lonely /ˈloʊn.li/ solitário(a) I feel lonely when…
connected /kəˈnɛk.tɪd/ conectado(a) Social media makes me feel connected to…
scrolling /ˈskroʊ.lɪŋ/ rolando (a tela) I spend too much time scrolling…
compare /kəmˈpɛr/ comparar I sometimes compare my life to…
face-to-face /ˌfeɪs.tə.ˈfeɪs/ presencial / cara a cara I prefer face-to-face conversations because…
follower /ˈfɑː.loʊ.ər/ seguidor(a) Having many followers doesn't mean…

2. Write 1 personal sentence using one of the words above.

Fase 3 — Diálogo
10 minutes

Dialogue

3 Read the dialogue out loud with a partner:

Lucas: I posted a photo yesterday and got 200 likes. It felt amazing!
Bianca: Really? I deleted my Instagram last month. I feel much better now.
Lucas: Deleted it? But how do you talk to your friends?
Bianca: I call them. Or I see them face-to-face. It's more real.
Lucas: I don't know… I think I would feel lonely without social media.
Bianca: That's the problem, Lucas. You feel lonely without the app — not without the people.

4. Write 1 sentence: do you agree with Lucas or Bianca? Why?

Fase 4 — Fala primeiro
15 minutes

Speak First — Then Write

  • 5 Do you use social media every day? Why or why not? A1
  • 6 Do you ever feel lonely after scrolling for a long time? What happens? A2
  • 7 Some people say social media is destroying real friendships. Do you agree or disagree? Explain. B1
Support Phrases — use these if you need help:

I think…  /  I don't think…  /  In my opinion…  /  I agree because…  /  I disagree because…

Fase 5 — Registro escrito
8 minutes

Written Record

8 Write 3 sentences about today's discussion. Use at least 2 words from Fase 2.

1. ___________________________________________________________

2. ___________________________________________________________

3. ___________________________________________________________

💡 Remember: use words like lonely, connected, scrolling, compare, face-to-face, or follower in your sentences!

Fase 6 — Fechamento
7 minutes

Closing

9 Tell the teacher one thing from today's class in English.
