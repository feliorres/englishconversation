English Conversation Lesson

If Everything Were Possible, What Would You Do?

Level: A1 – B1 • Time: 55 min
Fase 1 — Hook
5 minutes
"If you could wake up tomorrow with one new ability, what would it be?"

What do you think? — Today we talk about this.

Fase 2 — Vocabulário-chave
10 minutes

Key Vocabulary

1 Read out loud and repeat after the teacher:

WordPronunciationMeaningUse it
would /wʊd/ faria / iria I would travel to…
dream /driːm/ sonho / sonhar My biggest dream is…
ability /əˈbɪl.ə.ti/ habilidade If I had the ability to…
imagine /ɪˈmædʒ.ɪn/ imaginar Imagine you could…
fly /flaɪ/ voar If I could fly, I would…
invisible /ɪnˈvɪz.ə.bəl/ invisível If I were invisible, I would…

2. Write 1 personal sentence using one of the words above.

Fase 3 — Diálogo
10 minutes

Dialogue

3 Read the dialogue out loud with a partner:

Sofia: If you could have any superpower, what would you choose?
Rafael: Easy. I would fly. Imagine — no traffic, no bus, just me and the sky.
Sofia: That's cool, but I would be invisible. I'd go anywhere without anyone knowing.
Rafael: That's a bit scary, Sofia. What would you do if nobody could see you?
Sofia: I would visit other countries for free. And maybe listen to what people really think about me.
Rafael: Hmm… maybe some things are better not knowing.

4. Write 1 sentence: do you agree with Sofia or Rafael? Why?

Fase 4 — Fala primeiro
15 minutes

Speak First — Then Write

  • 5 If you could visit any country, where would you go? Why? A1
  • 6 If you had one superpower — to fly or to be invisible — which one would you choose? Use one vocabulary word from Fase 2. A2
  • 7 Some people say: "If everything were possible, life would be boring." Do you agree? Why or why not? B1
Support Phrases — use these if you need help:

I think…  /  I don't think…  /  In my opinion…  /  I agree because…  /  I disagree because…  /  If I could… I would…

Fase 5 — Registro escrito
8 minutes

Written Record

8 Write 3 sentences about today's discussion. Use at least 2 words from Fase 2.

1. ___________________________________________________________

2. ___________________________________________________________

3. ___________________________________________________________

💡 Remember: use words like would, dream, ability, imagine, fly, or invisible in your sentences!

Fase 6 — Fechamento
7 minutes

Closing

9 Tell the teacher one thing from today's class in English.
